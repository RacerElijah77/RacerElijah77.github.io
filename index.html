- About Me -

My name is Elijah Gasataya, but you can call me EJ for short! I am currently an undergraduate student at The College of New Jersey (TCNJ) majoring in computer science. Not too long ago I found great interest in front-end web development because I always loved to somehow link my creative ability with the programming skills and concepts I've learned throughout my academia.

In relation to my creative ability, I also love to produce music. My alias, EJGSTY has been with me since 2016- my freshman year of high school. I also love to play video games during my free time (like there is such a thing nowadays)! I play games from a wide array of genres, such as Battlefield, Dying Light, and Assassin's Creed.
